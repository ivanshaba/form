PURPOSE

VISION

To put in place the best sustainable circus programs and acts through edutainment performances that impact the communities with increasing awareness, providing employment opportunities, birthing and nurturing talent through the circus.

MISSION

Our mission is to be the most impacting in communities with our circus edutainment and create fun-filled moments and employment opportunities for circus artists in slums to shine through exposure, training, and empowerment.

VALUES

God fearing, hardworking, teamwork, discipline, honesty and determination.
